# High-Converting Landing Page Architecture

This skill provides a strict 8-step architectural framework for creating high-converting landing pages. Whenever generating the copy, layout, or structure for a landing page, ensure all 8 sections are included in this exact order.

## 1. HERO (The Hook)
**Job:** Get the user's email or get them to scroll down.
**Structure:**
- Eyebrow (Short contextual intro)
- Headline (Clear, impactful promise)
- Subheadline (How it works or who it's for)
- CTA (Call to action button above the fold)
- Trust Signals (Logos, star ratings, or short credibility metrics)

## 2. SUCCESS (Conditional)
**Job:** Kill buyer's remorse and reassure the user.
**Structure:**
- Checkmark Icon
- Confirmation Statement
- Deliverable List (What they get immediately)

## 3. PROBLEM-AGITATE
**Job:** Make the status quo feel painful.
**Structure:**
- 3 Specific Problems with Agitation
- Personal Transition (Bridge from problem to the solution)

## 4. VALUE STACK
**Job:** Make saying "no" feel stupid by stacking undeniable value.
**Structure:**
- 4 Tiers of Value (Descending order)
- Total Value Calculation (e.g., "Total Value: $X")
- Your Price ("Today: $Y")

## 5. SOCIAL PROOF
**Job:** Let others convince the prospect.
**Structure:**
- Section Header
- 3 Testimonials (Must include specific results, names, and ideally photos or roles)

## 6. TRANSFORMATION
**Job:** Make the final outcome tangible and clear.
**Structure:**
- 4 Stages: 
  1. Quick Win
  2. Compound Effect
  3. The Unfair Advantage
  4. 10x Result

## 7. SECONDARY CTA
**Job:** Catch the scrollers who skipped to the bottom.
**Structure:**
- Avatar Stack (Faces of users/customers)
- Question Headline (e.g., "Ready to get started?")
- "Yes" Button (Clear and final CTA)

## 8. FOOTER
**Job:** Establish professional legitimacy.
**Structure:**
- Company Logo
- Navigation Links
- Legal (Privacy Policy, Terms of Service)
- Social Media Links
